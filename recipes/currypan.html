Curry Pan

Description

Curry... but in a bun

Ingredients

For the curry

  • 15g (1/2oz) butter
  • 1/2 small onion diced
  • 10g (1/2oz) plain all-purpose flour
  • 10g (1/2oz) curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 120ml (1/2cup) vegetable stock
  • 1/2 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 80g (3oz) cauliflower, cut into small pieces
  • 20g (1/4cup) peas
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ketchup
  • hot chili sauce, to taste (optional)

For the dough

  • 5g (1 teaspoon) instant yeast
  • 60ml (1/4cup) lukewarm milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 250g (2cups) strong white bread flour
  • 50g (1/3cup) plain all-purpose flour
  • 3g (1/2 teaspoon) salt
  • 15g (1 tablespoon) caster sugar
  • 80g (3oz) butter, softened and cut into small pieces

To assemble and serve

  • 1 egg, beaten with a splash of water or milk
  • 40g (1-1/2oz) panko
  • vegetable oil, for deep frying (about 1.5 litres or 6 cups)

Directions

  1. To make the curry, melt the butter in a small saucepan, add the onion and fry for 5 minutes or until golden brown
  2. Stir in the flour, curry powder, and garam masala, then cook the roux for a few minutes, stirring constantly
  3. Add the stock and bring to a boil. Add the carrot, cauliflower, and peas and cook until tender, stirring frequently to ensure the sauce doesn't catch
  4. Stir in the soy sauce, ketchup, and chili sauce (if using) then remove from heat and chill thoroughly
  5. To make the dough, stir together the yeast and milk until the yeast dissolves, then stir the mixture into the eggs
  6. Place the flours, salt, and sugar in a mixing bowl and mix lightly, then add the liquid ingredients
  7. Mix by hand or on low speed for 2 minutes, then turn the speed up to medium and mix for another 7 minutes or knead on a floured surface for 15 minutes
  8. Add the butter and knead or mix for a further 5 minutes until no chunks of butter remain and the dough is very smooth and soft
  9. Wrap the dough in a plastic wrap and chill for at least 2 hours
  10. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces and roll them into balls
