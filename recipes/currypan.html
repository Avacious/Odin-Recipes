Curry Pan

Description

Curry... but in a bun

Ingredients

For the curry

  • 15g (1/2oz) butter
  • 1/2 small onion diced
  • 10g (1/2oz) plain all-purpose flour
  • 10g (1/2oz) curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 120ml (1/2cup) vegetable stock
  • 1/2 carrot, peeled and diced
  • 80g (3oz) cauliflower, cut into small pieces
  • 20g (1/4cup) peas
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ketchup
  • hot chili sauce, to taste (optional)

For the dough

  • 5g (1 teaspoon) instant yeast
  • 60ml (1/4cup) lukewarm milk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 250g (2cups) strong white bread flour
  • 50g (1/3cup) plain all-purpose flour
  • 3g (1/2 teaspoon) salt
  • 15g (1 tablespoon) caster sugar
  • 80g (3oz) butter, softened and cut into small pieces

To assemble and serve

  • 1 egg, beaten with a splash of water or milk
  • 40g (1-1/2oz) panko
  • vegetable oil, for deep frying (about 1.5 litres or 6 cups)
