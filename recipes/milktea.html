Milk Tea

Description

extremely rich milk tea made by infusing the tea directly into the milk instead of adding a splash of milk to already infused tea

Ingredients

  • 1 litre (4 cups) whole milk
  • 4 Black tea bags
  • 4-6 Tablespoons golden caster sugar

Directions

  1. Place 250ml (1 cup) of milk in saucepan along with sugar and tea bags
  2. Bring to a simmer, stir to dissolve the sugar
  3. Remove from heat and add remaining milk
  4. Transfer to the fridge and leave to infuse for 2 hours
  5. Remove tea bags
  6. keep bottled in fridge for up to 3 days. Serve chilled
