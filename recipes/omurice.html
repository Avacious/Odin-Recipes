Omurice

Description

Every anime girls favorite meal to make her "Senpai" or "Onii-chan"

Ingredients

  • 30g (1oz) butter
  • 1 banana shallot or small onion, diced
  • 60g (2oz) shiitake mushrooms, destemmed and diced
  • 1 chicken thigh, boneless and skinless, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 300g (1-2/3cups) cooked rice
  • ketchup, to taste plus extra to serve
  • soy sauce, to taste
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 3 eggs, beaten

Directions

  1. Melt half of the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat, then saute the shallots or onion until translucent
  2. Add the shiitake and the chicken and saute until the mushrooms soften and the chicken is cooked through
  3. Add the rice, breaking up any clumps, and stir in the ketchup, soy sauce, salt and pepper
  4. Meanwhile, melt the remaining butter in a non-stick frying pan over a medium-high heat, then tip in the beaten eggs and season with a little salt
  5. Cook egg until set on the bottom but still runny on top, then gently fold the eggs over themselves so the runny bit is now in the middle
  6. Scoop the fried rice into a mound on a plate, then tip the omelette onto the top of the rice. Serve with more ketchup, if you like
